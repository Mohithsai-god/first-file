Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

Summary

Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba follows the journey of Tanjiro Kamado, a kind-hearted boy who becomes a demon slayer after his family is slaughtered by demons, leaving his sister Nezuko transformed into a demon. Determined to avenge his family and restore Nezuko's humanity, Tanjiro joins the Demon Slayer Corps, facing numerous challenges and formidable foes along the way.
